## Understand

- [ ] `not assessed` — **Where treasury stock lives on the balance sheet**: Dollar amount on face; share count in the parenthetical description. Also appears in Statement of Equity. → **P1**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Journal entry for share repurchase**: Debit Treasury Stock (at cost), Credit Cash. Where to locate the cost: Statement of Equity OR Cash Flow Financing section OR Note 16. → **P2**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Stock-based compensation journal entry**: DR Compensation Expense, CR Additional Paid-in-Capital. Why APIC and not cash. Locate annual expense in Note 18 / Cash Flow Statement / Statement of Equity. → **P4**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Unrecognized compensation expense**: What the "total unrecognized compensation expense" figure represents (remaining grant-date fair value to be expensed over remaining service period). Located in Note 18 RSU section. → **P3**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Straight-line amortization of comp expense**: Unrecognized expense divided by weighted-average remaining period = annual expense. Edge case: remaining period < 1 year. → **P3**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Tax treatment of stock options (book vs. tax)**: Book records grant-date fair value over vesting; tax deduction = intrinsic value at exercise. Why they differ. → **P6**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Avoidable vs. unavoidable fixed costs**: In discontinuation/make-buy, only avoidable costs are relevant. Indirect fixed costs typically continue. → **P7, P9**

## Apply

- [ ] `not assessed` — **Locate treasury stock share count (fiscal 2025)**: Read the balance-sheet Class A common stock parenthetical ("X million and Y million shares issued; … outstanding") OR Treasury Stock shares column in Statement of Equity. → **P1**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Pull share-repurchase cost for a specific fiscal year**: Cross-reference Statement of Equity ("Repurchases of common stock" row), Cash Flow Statement financing section, and Note 16 (excludes excise tax caveat). → **P2**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Compute next-year comp expense from footnote**: Unrecognized expense ÷ weighted-average remaining period, but cap at remaining period if <1 year (straight-line over remaining). → **P3**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Build aggregate stock-comp JE from disclosed expense**: Use the Note 18 "Compensation expense" line (fiscal 2025) as debit; APIC as credit. → **P4**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Compute total fair value of RSUs granted**: Number of shares granted × weighted-average grant-date fair value per share. Watch units (thousands vs. millions). → **P5**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Discontinuation impact**: Lost contribution margin = (price − variable cost per unit) × units. If all fixed costs are indirect/unavoidable, pre-tax income changes by the negative of lost CM. → **P7**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Multi-product breakeven with sales mix**: Build weighted-average CM per "bundle" at the given mix, divide fixed costs by bundle CM, then allocate bundle count back to individual products. → **P8**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Make vs. buy**: Compare (variable cost to make + avoidable fixed OH) × units vs. (purchase price × units). Don't include unavoidable fixed OH. → **P9**
- [ ] `not assessed` — **Process-further decision**: Incremental revenue − incremental variable cost − incremental fixed cost = advantage (if positive). → **P10**
